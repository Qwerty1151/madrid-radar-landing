Product

Madrid Radar is a workflow tool for professionals who track Madrid Monitor trademark cases. It keeps a clean portfolio view, a case timeline of meaningful updates, and exports.

What it helps with

  • Portfolio view: cases and designated countries at a glance
  • Event timeline: key updates, statuses, and documents in one place
  • Faster detection of changes ("early signals") on paid plans
  • Exports for reporting and internal workflows

Who it is for

  • Trademark attorneys
  • IP paralegals / assistants
  • In-house IP teams
  • IP consultants managing international portfolios

Data and positioning

Madrid Radar is a complementary analytics layer around Madrid Monitor workflows. We aim to present portfolio information in a clearer, more actionable format.
