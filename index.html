What is Madrid Radar?

Madrid Radar is a legal monitoring service designed for trademark attorneys, IP consultants, and companies managing international trademark portfolios. The service provides structured access to trademark registration events published in the official WIPO Madrid Monitor system.

What does the service do?

Madrid Radar helps professionals:

  • monitor changes and events in international trademark registrations;
  • track legal status updates across designated jurisdictions;
  • review structured timelines of trademark-related events;
  • work with publicly available registry data in a convenient analytical format.

Data sources

Madrid Radar uses publicly available information published by the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) in the Madrid Monitor system. The service does not modify, bypass, or restrict access to any official resources and is intended as a complementary analytical tool.

Who is this service for?

  • Trademark attorneys
  • Intellectual property consultants
  • Legal professionals
  • Companies managing trademark portfolios
